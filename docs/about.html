üìà TRADING STRATEGIES

Our system employs 10 sophisticated trading strategies:

  1. Momentum Strategy: Identifies stocks with strong price trends
  2. Mean Reversion: Exploits temporary price deviations
  3. Volume Breakout: Detects unusual volume spikes
  4. Technical Indicators: Combines RSI, MACD, and Bollinger Bands
  5. Pattern Recognition: Identifies chart patterns
  6. Volatility Arbitrage: Trades volatility expansions/contractions
  7. Moving Average Crossover: Classic trend-following approach
  8. Support/Resistance: Trades key price levels
  9. Market Sentiment: Analyzes market psychology indicators
  10. Ensemble Method: Combines signals from multiple strategies

üîÑ HOW IT WORKS

1. Data Collection: Every trading day, the system fetches the latest market data including price, volume, and technical indicators for the top 10 stocks.

2. Signal Generation: Each strategy independently analyzes the data and generates trading signals (LONG, SHORT, or HOLD) with confidence scores.

3. Position Management: Trades are tracked from entry to exit with automated monitoring of price targets and stop-loss levels.

4. Performance Tracking: All trades are logged and analyzed to calculate performance metrics and improve strategy parameters.
